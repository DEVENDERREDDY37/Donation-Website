ABOUT US

At IIIT Bhagalpur, we are committed to eradicating poverty through education, innovation, and community engagement. Our mission is to provide equal opportunities and access to education, healthcare, and vocational training for underserved communities in Bhagalpur and beyond. We envision a future where everyone can break free from poverty's cycle and lead lives of dignity. We collaborate with local partners, NGOs, and government agencies to ensure the maximum impact of our initiatives. Our college's diverse community shares a strong commitment to social responsibility. We have a proven track record of successful poverty ' alleviation projects in Bhagalpur. Join us in creating a brighter, more equitable future for all.

Adopt A Child & Save Life

"Adopt a Child and Save a Life" is an initiative urging individuals and families to provide lovi ng, stable homes through adoption. Adoption offers children in adversity a permanent, caring family. It rescues them from neglect, abuse, or abandonment, provi ding love and security. It involves a legal process, fostering strong bonds, and enriching both child and parent lives. Adoption comes in various forms, meeting diverse needs. Advoca tes promote awareness to combat stigmas. By adopting, individuals positively impact a child's future. It's a powerful way to make a difference in a child's life and build a loving family.
